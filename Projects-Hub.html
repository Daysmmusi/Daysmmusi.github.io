Research Title: AI-Optimized Smart Grid Systems for Seamless Integration of Distributed Renewable Energy Sources​ 
Date Published: 04-04-25
Author: Firstname Lastname
Student ID: 21000123
Course: BEng Electrical & Electronic

Summary: This research explores the application of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms to enhance the stability and efficiency of smart grids in managing distributed renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind. The study focuses on developing real-time predictive models to balance supply-demand fluctuations, mitigate grid congestion, and optimize energy storage utilization. Project Spotlight

Research Title: ​CRISPR-Cas12a Mediated Metabolic Engineering of Soil Microbes for Enhanced Carbon Sequestration and Soil Health​ 
Date Published: 13-03-25
Author: Firstname Lastname
Student ID: 24000123
Course: BSc Biology & Biotechnology

Summary: ​This study investigates the use of CRISPR-Cas12a gene-editing tools to engineer soil microbial communities for improved carbon capture and agricultural sustainability. By modifying metabolic pathways in bacteria and fungi, the research aims to enhance their ability to convert atmospheric CO₂ into stable soil organic carbon while promoting nutrient cycling and drought resilience in crops. The project combines synthetic biology, metagenomics, and field trials to assess the ecological impact and scalability of these bioengineered microbes. Successful implementation could offer a dual solution to climate change and food insecurity, transforming agricultural practices in arid regions like Sub-Saharan Africa.
